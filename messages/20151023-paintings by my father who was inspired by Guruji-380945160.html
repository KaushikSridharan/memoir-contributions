 Mandar, 
This refers to our conversation about my father and his paintings. My father was one of the 'old masters' of Indian artists, closely associated with what was then called the Progressive Artists Group in Mumbai that consisted of artists like Gaitonde, Ara, Hussain, D'Souza. 

He held various exhibitions of his paintings, and his favorite subject was Yoga and what he learnt from Guruji. I am attaching his brochure, where he has mentioned it. Also attached are some of his paintings with the yoga as the theme. 

A little about him and the Institute: 
When he came to Pune, he joined the institute in 1978 and was absolutely in awe of Guruji. He used to come to the institute once a week. However since his work took him to Chennai and Bangalore, he could not come. However he made me and my mother join, then. 

He had two copies of Guruji's book  - Light on Yoga. One, which he used while painting and the other when he did his asanas in the morning. 
He performed asanas till the day he passed away and attributed his good health to Yoga and what that he learnt from Guruji.
