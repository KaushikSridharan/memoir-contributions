Guruji was so in the moment that he could be especially compassionate with children.  I loved to watch how he would work with Guarika.  She, being so precocious,  was filled with enthusiasm when Guruji was there to work with her, and would accept almost any challenge he brought to her.  Her delight in yoga met his, to removed the physical, psychological barriers so that he was spiritually connected to her.   In a small aside, he asked what I saw in her, and he allowed me to see that she had up-liftment from inside.  She was very happy to know that it was not only her experience, but that he could understand that in her, and that I could feel that through observations he wanted us to see.  He was that kind of extraordinary teacher that brought you right into the present moment.  Alone, I would not have had that keenness of observation, but he made us keen.

Laurel Thomas Laulkar
