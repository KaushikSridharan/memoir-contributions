Namaste Geeta Ma'am and Prashant sir,
I am grateful to you for giving me the opportunity to express my gratitude and respect for Guruji in the form of the poem below.

I read the notice board today morning and as it was raining I had to wait after class and these words came to me in the inspiring premises of our institute.

I fully understand that the publication of the poem depends on various factors but irrespective of it being published or not I feel priviledged to share with you my feelings towards Guruji.

With loads of gratitude
Rashmi

Immortal Inspiration

In the darkest dungeon and in the brightest light

You stand as an impenetrable, eternal ray

Offering the wealth of healthy insight.

 

Like the roots of a tree you went deep to the core

Of this overwhelming science to tirelessly explore

And then unraveled secrets for people… eager or sore.

 

Guruji my body was a piece of misshaped clay

Before the radiance of your wisdom lighted my way.

Whenever in life I face a mind-boggling situation

I roll my shoulders back keeping my chest erect from the sternum

A change in my posture changes my attitude

And this makes my heart flood with gratitude

 

Your death never ever filled me with grief

For your life will keep providing relief

And even Death must have died to take

A life so genuine in a world so fake….

A soul like yours never ever dies

I see you on Sunday mornings …

In the children’s eyes.

 

I see you on Sunday mornings …

In the children’s eyes.


by Rashmi
