Dear Geeta,  Prashant and Smriti Chawla:

Last summer I started to make sculptures inspired by Light on Yoga. They are made ​​of terra cotta and glazed. 
The relief playing with the contrast between glazed and unglazed. The body shape and size are taken from the book Light on Yoga.
The sculptures are based on my own body but always from Guruji image's doing asanas.
I am proud to participate with my humble cooperation. Thank you for providing this great opportunity.
Sincerely
Tanit Fariña I'm 40 years old. I'm Iyengar Yoga student for 13 years from Jordi Marti's Barcelona center .
I'm so glad to schedule next summer in Ramamani Iyengar Memorial Yoga Institute to continue inspiration with my sculptures and paintings.
