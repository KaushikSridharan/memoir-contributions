Guruji gave intense generosity and generous intensity!
This is thought that came to the heart the first time at the Institute in 1995. Guruji had given me a certain way to do Sirsasana with a trifold mat and then folded again -a six fold!  Balance on that was a must and somehow became steady when he was there. Two days later in the pose he walks by and says " Who told you to do that high!" What choice but to say nothing , feel the play and give a respectful chuckle to one's self!  He said, "enough now 1/2 of that"and gave the famous one eyebrow up smile. The moment in playful connection remains imprinted in the cave of this heart.
" Go with courage and caution." Guruji would tell me when taking leave from him to go home.
