Dear Geeta and Prashant,
my name es Elisabetta Pescucci and I’m an “Introductury 2” teacher in Italy . I’ve never been in Pune in your place and I never met your father in person, but I would like to share what happened to me the day of his passing.
            Last spring I decided to open my own teaching space for the Iyengar yoga lessons and so I rented a room and started preparing it for the lessons. Since I did not have enough money for all the work that had to be done, I did some of it on my own. On August 20th while I was painting the walls of my new little studio, full of hope that everything will be well, I received a phone call with the information that your father had passed. I immediately went on the internet to read about it and found out that just before his passing he had said “the end will be your beginning”.
            I was thinking about what I was doing and how in that very moment I was beginning my life as a yoga teacher while thousands of people were teaching, studying and practicing Iyengar yoga. It was a very touching moment, full of joy. From that moment on my uncertainties had vanished and I feel that Guruji, even though not in physical form any more, is sustaining my path.
 
Thank you for reading my message and blessings to all of us, may we walk in beauty in Guruji’s memory
 
                                            Elisabetta Pescucci
