Sitting on a orange cushion in black and flesh
Hearing sounds in Sanskrit chanted 
A male figure robes of orange
I hear cadences of Vrindavan where devotion Krsna
Themed the land the trees the skies

A portrait of passed Guru garlanded in flowers
Blessed water blessed fire blessed flower petals
Sits a man in robes of office in black silk full of devotion
To art and craft yoga of a Guru recently passed

I leaned into robed walls of wooden strength
Hearing cadences of Vrindavan floating Krsna in New York air
Chants that I know not words I know not realizing Krsna floating 
Heart blessing and heart gift to a passed Guru

My glass covered eyes leaks soft silent tears right eye
Obeying convictions of decorum my heart floating Krsna
Self and I in robes of dreamweaver weaving oneness us
Quietly my body vibrates in cadences floating Krsna

Krsna manifested in heart offering of others
Sitting in chairs in orange cushions or standing in a room filled
Krsna floating in a gift to a passed Guru garlanded
