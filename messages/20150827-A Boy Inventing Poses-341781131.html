Yvonne Caro Caro is a Certified Iyengar Yoga Teacher who graduated from the prestigious Iyengar Yoga Teacher Training Program in Los Angeles. She has been practicing Hatha Yoga since 1991 and has been intensively studying Iyengar Yoga since 2000, with a particular interest on its therapeutic applications. 

 

Following the teachings of yoga pioneer BKS Iyengar, Caro Caro creates a nurturing, challenging and playful environment that gives special attention to the unique physical needs and abilities of each student. 

 

Currently, Yvonne teaches privates and group classes. She taught yoga to medical students at UCLA’s David Geffen School of Medicine, and gives regular workshops overlooking the Atlantic Ocean in Rincon, Puerto Rico at the beach resort, Hotel Villa Cofresi.  She has taught in the Theater Department at UCLA’s School of Theater, Film and Television, and been on the dramatic arts faculty at the University of Puerto Rico, Rio Piedras. Prior to focusing on Yoga, she practiced, studied and taught acting, dance, pantomime and movement for actors as a student and professional artist for more than twenty years. This background in theatre and film supports the acute observation skills she applies while teaching both in class settings and with private students.

 

Yvonne just got back from her first trip to Pune, India to study with Master B.K.S. Iyengar, Prashant Iyengar and Dr. Geeta Iyengar.

 

Caro Caro holds an MFA from UCLA’s School of Theater, Film and Television.
