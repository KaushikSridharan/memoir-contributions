At the 80th Birthday Celebration, I happened to be in the store, and  I wanted to purchase some books. There was only one copy of Gem for Women, and Tree of Yoga, so I asked if those were the last copies and if I could purchase them. 

From behind me, I heard: "if it's the last copy, it's double the price." Startled, I turned, to find Guruji smiling behind me and with a sparkle in his eyes. I responded, "If you autograph them for me, I will pay double!" And he went on to autograph them, with a big, sweet grin. 

We then had a wonderful conversation about the World Health Organization, and how yoga was so important as preventative health.
