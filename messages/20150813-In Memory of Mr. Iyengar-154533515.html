Greetings

In honor and in gratitude on the occasion of his 95th season in 2013 I sent this piece to Pune addressed to Mr. Iyengar

 

The following list is some of why it made sense to me to give this artwork to him

 

Beauty in the broken

The imperfect and the flawed

Highlighting the internal

The light within

Spiraling

Knowledge Growing

Measure

The sound of the sea

Blue for communication

His keen gift of word and language

Violet for the crown

 

Nancy Brooks Brody

New York City 2015
