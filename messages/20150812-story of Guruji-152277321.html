In 1984 at the end of the First International Iyengar Yoga Convention. I sat with Guruji (Mr. Iyengar at the time). and tried to pay him for the profits on his sold out demonstration at Davies symphony hall. We had the loosest of agreements for his attendance at the convention, only offering to cover his airfare to the U.S.. 

After his demonstration I attempted to give him the ticket sale profits of $27,00.00 to Guruji.
He informed me he would not accept it saying it was not his but should remain in San Francisco to help promote yoga in the U.S..

Such was the nature of this man.
