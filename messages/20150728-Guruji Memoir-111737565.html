Dear friends at RIMYI,
 
Irish Blessing for Guruji & Bellur Fundraiser/92nd Birthday Celebrations
 
I am happy to share the fond memories I have of presenting the attached Irish blessing to Guruji in the library at RIMYI during my visit there in January 2011.  I also had a donation for the Bellur Trust to give to him from the East Clare Yoga Centre in Ireland which had been raised by yoga students during a charity outdoor yoga event we held in December 2010 to mark the occasion of Gurujiâ€™s 92nd Birthday and raise funds for Bellur.  I showed him some of the photos from the event and some of the press coverage that was generated.  He seemed to really enjoy seeing these (see attached).   I have higher resolution images of the photographs if you want to re-print any of them.
 
Sharing Details of the inaugural National Iyengar Yoga Day in Ireland, February 2015
 
As Chair of the Munster Iyengar Yoga Institute in Ireland I was delighted to send details of National Iyengar Yoga Day to Guruji in February 2014.  Our members were touched to receive a letter back from him saying that it was nice for him to read about the event.  We will treasure this letter.
 
Thank you for putting together this memoir and giving us the opportunity to revisit these precious memories.  His legacy burns brightly around the world and I personally feel his presence in my heart each day.
 
Warm regards from Ireland.
