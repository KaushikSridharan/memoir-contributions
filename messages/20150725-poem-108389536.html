I bring my heart and start to write my writing 
I take a lot of photos in my life and want to share with everyone . My heart is full of love and enjoy my life .
I follow the rhythm of nature to dance. I hear the sounds of water to do meditation. I stretch my body under the sun .
My pen is my hand . When I start to write my writing and I prepare to do my best to record every moment of my life .
